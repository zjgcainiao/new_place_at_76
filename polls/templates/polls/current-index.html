About Us

We are the neighborhood friendly auto repair shop, which is known for our quick friendly oil change Service and repair service.  Stop by and try us out. During Covid-19 period, the shop closes at Sundays temporarily. 

We appreciate sincerely that our customers feel respected, comfortable and put their trusts in us. Our mechanics take the time to help our customers understand what the issues are with the vehicles. We might not be the cheapest mechanic shop in town, but we are loyal to our supporting customers and put 100% into our jobs.

We understand that finding an reliable auto Repair shop is often frustrating and time-consuming. That is why we are here to help. We want to make you feel like family.
